Waste Hunters

PITCH

In this simple yet addictive dropper game, players take control of a quirky little box character named Boxey as they navigate a sky full of falling trash bags. The objective is to skillfully dodge the falling garbage for as long as possible, testing your reflexes and precision. The longer you survive, the faster the bags fall, turning up the challenge and excitement. What makes this game so lovable is its simplicity—easy to pick up, but hard to master. Players are drawn in by Boxey’s charm, the smooth controls, and the satisfying thrill of weaving through the falling debris. It’s a game that proves you don’t need flashy graphics or complex mechanics to deliver pure fun!

THEME

The theme of the game centers around saving the planet from pollution, with Boxey taking on the role of an unlikely hero in a world overwhelmed by trash. As trash bags rain down from the sky, Boxey’s mission is to avoid getting buried under the waste, symbolizing the struggle to rise above the growing pollution problem. Each second the player survives represents a small victory in the fight for a cleaner Earth. This simple gameplay is tied to a powerful message: even the smallest actions—like dodging a trash bag—can raise awareness about keeping our planet clean. It’s a fun and meaningful way to remind players that everyone, even a little box like Boxey, can be part of the solution.

MY WHY

I made this game to spread awareness about pollution in a fun and interactive way. I wanted to show that even simple games can carry a powerful message and inspire players to care more about protecting the environment.
